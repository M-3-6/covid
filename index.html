GENERAL AWARENESS

SYMPTOMS

  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Tiredness
  • Trouble breathing
  • Sore throat

SPREAD

The virus spreads mainly through respiratory
droplets of an infected person

TREATMENT

People who get a mild disease need
care to ease their symptoms

PREVENTIVE MEASURES

Wash your hands often with soap
and water or clean them with
an alcohol-based sanitizer

Cover your nose
and mouth in public

Practice social
distancing
